    About Us

    Helping you reach your mobility Goals

    Eyethu Prosthetics and Orthotics Inc is a South African Company owned by Yamkela Didiza, it specialises in Prosthetics and Orthotics but their line of work is not only limited to that, it involves pretty much everything that has to do with rehabilitation medicine. With the heart of the company dedicated to helping and serving others, we provide our services directly to our customers.

    We are dedicated to empowering you with the tools, support, and confidence to lead an active and independent life. At Eyethu Prosthetics and Orthotics Inc, we don’t just create solutions—we create partnerships that help you thrive.

    At Eyethu Prosthetics and Orthotics Inc, we specialize in transforming lives through innovative prosthetics, orthotics, and rehabilitation medicine. Our mission is to empower individuals by restoring mobility, independence, and confidence.

    • Custom-designed prosthetics that enhance mobility and fit seamlessly into your lifestyle.
    • Precision-crafted orthotic devices for improved comfort and support.
    • Comprehensive rehabilitation services that prioritize your unique recovery journey.

    We are committed to providing compassionate, client-focused care. Let Eyethu Prosthetics and Orthotics Inc support you every step of the way toward a better tomorrow.
